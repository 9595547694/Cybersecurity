IAAA

Identification, authentication, authorization, auditing and Accountability.

Identification - Unique username

Authentication - Verify username using password, PassPin, accesscard, token, biometric, location

Types - SFA, MFA

Authorization - Rights and Permission

Access control models
MAC - Mandatory Access Control
RBAC - Role Based Access Control
Rule based access Control
DAC- Descriptionary Access Control


Auditing - Monitoring of actions, events, recording the events. collection of logs of systems so to detect malicious activities.

Accountability - "The priniciple that an individual is entrusted to safeguard and control equipment keying material, & info is answerable to proper authority for the loss or misue of that info" - NIST. Reviewing the log file to check for compliance.
